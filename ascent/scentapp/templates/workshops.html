Our Perfume Workshops

Step into the enchanting world of fragrance creation with our exclusive Perfume Workshop. This immersive experience is designed to awaken your senses and guide you through the intricate process of blending unique scents.

Over the course of 90 minutes, you will learn about the origins of perfume, explore a wide range of essential oils and aromas, and craft your very own signature scent to take home.

The workshop is held exclusively on Sundays, providing a serene atmosphere to relax and unleash your creativity.

Whether you're a fragrance enthusiast or a curious beginner, this workshop offers a unique opportunity to dive into the fascinating world of perfumery.
